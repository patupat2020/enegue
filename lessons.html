Labelling Activity

  This activity is a great way to exercise your memory. Start the activity by turning two cards over at random by clicking on the card. If they are a match, then you are correct and go again. If they are a no match, it will turn back over, and you need to look for match again. As more and more cards are revealed you should remember where certain cards are in the grid. Win the game by pairing all the cards correctly.
